HOW DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION THROUGH COOKIES?


Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activities on our services. Cookies record information about your online preferences and contain information by which LUNAR can identify your computer to our servers. We may obtain information about your general internet usage by using a cookie file which is stored on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. We may also collect information about your computer, including where available your IP address (an IP address is a number that can uniquely identify a specific computer or other network device on the internet), operating system, browser type and analysis software. LUNAR endeavours to collect personal information about an individual only from that individual. In some circumstances LUNAR may obtain personal information from a third party. LUNAR may use information it collects from you for the primary purpose for which it is collected and for such other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose of collection. Many websites do this, because cookies enable website publishers to do useful things like find out whether the device (and probably its user) has visited the website before. This is done on a repeat visit by checking to see, and finding, the cookie left there on the last visit. LUNAR may use information contained in cookies to make assumptions about the user of the computer and to provide users of that computer with focused advertising that LUNAR believes may be of interest, based on that information. In some cases a web page might contain more than one cookie, The information gathered from these different cookies are used to build statistical and demographical information which does not identify you personally to advertisers and other third parties for marketing, promotional purposes. Please note that our advertisers may also use cookies, over which we have no control. Users have the opportunity to set their devices to accept all cookies to notify them when a cookie is issued, or not to receive cookies at any time. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may be unable to access certain parts of our website. Cookies allow you to take full advantage of some of LUNAR’s features, and we recommend that you leave them turned on. Moreover, certain personalised services cannot then be provided to you and accordingly you may not be able to take full advantage of our features.
